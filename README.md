# Newsletter / Email Marketing for B2B SaaS Research

## Objective

This project researches leading practitioners in newsletter and email marketing for B2B SaaS companies. The goal is to understand how successful operators use newsletters, email marketing, content distribution, audience building, and lifecycle marketing to drive growth.

## Why I Chose This Topic

Newsletter and email marketing remain among the highest ROI growth channels for B2B SaaS companies. By studying practitioners who actively build newsletters, communities, and SaaS businesses, I can identify strategies that support customer acquisition, engagement, retention, and long-term growth.

## Key Learnings

* Personalized onboarding emails improve activation and conversion.
* Consistent content distribution is essential for newsletter growth.
* Community building strengthens audience engagement and retention.
* SEO and content marketing remain important acquisition channels.
* Customer psychology improves messaging and conversion rates.
* B2B SaaS growth benefits from combining email, content, and audience-building strategies.

## Conclusion

The selected experts provide a diverse view of B2B SaaS newsletter and email marketing, covering lifecycle marketing, content-led growth, audience building, customer psychology, community-led growth, and demand generation. Their insights create a strong foundation for developing a future B2B SaaS email marketing playbook.
